# Coffee-Shop-Sales
The Coffee Shop Sales dataset contains transactional sales records from a coffee shop, providing detailed information about customer purchases and business performance. Each record represents a single sales transaction and includes attributes such as transaction date and time, product category, product type, product name, unit price, quantity sold, and total sales amount.


### Project Overview

This project analyzes sales transactions from a coffee shop to uncover meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis focuses on understanding sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and revenue trends through interactive dashboards and visualizations.

Customer satisfaction: This primary dataset used for this analysis is the “COFEE-SET-EXCEL file, containing details information about each customer experience and feedback.

  ### Challenges Encountered

During this project, several challenges were encountered:

- Missing and inconsistent data that required cleaning before analysis.
- Incorrect data types for some columns, requiring conversion to appropriate formats.
- Duplicate records that had to be identified and removed.
- Inconsistent formatting of dates and text values.
- Difficulty creating relationships between tables during data modeling.
- Ensuring accurate KPI calculations across different reporting periods.
- Designing an interactive dashboard that was both informative and easy to understand.


### Solutions Implemented

The following solutions were applied to address the challenges:

- Cleaned and transformed the dataset using Power Query.
- Corrected data types for numerical, date, and text fields.
- Removed duplicate records and handled missing values appropriately.
- Standardized date formats and text entries for consistency.
- Validated calculations to ensure accurate KPIs and reports.
- Designed an interactive dashboard with slicers, charts, and KPI to improve user experience.
  

  ### Customer Satisfaction Report

The analysis provides valuable insights into customer purchasing behavior and overall business performance. Although the dataset does not contain direct customer feedback or ratings, customer satisfaction can be inferred from sales patterns.


### Summary

* High-performing products indicate strong customer demand and satisfaction.
* Consistent sales across multiple periods suggest customer loyalty and repeat purchases.
* Peak sales hours identify the times when customer engagement is highest.
* Popular product categories reflect customer preferences and purchasing trends.
* Store performance comparisons highlight locations that deliver stronger customer experiences.

### Recommendations

* Maintain adequate stock levels for best-selling products to prevent shortages.
* Introduce promotions during off-peak hours to increase customer traffic.
* Regularly review sales trends to adapt product offerings to customer preferences.
* Monitor inventory to ensure popular items remain available.
* Continue using sales dashboards to track performance and support data-driven business decisions.
